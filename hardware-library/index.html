Save money, reduce waste and share!

The DevNQ Hardware Library is part of a growing movement of share-economy lending libraries. The goal is to reduce the amount of e-waste created for future generations, save our borrowers money and even enable broader access to specialised tools and equipment that may be out of reach of some.

We achieve this by sharing equipment amongst our community, reducing the number of single-use or limited-use equipment purchases, and by repurposing equipment destined for landfill. You don't need to buy equipment, you'll use once, borrow it instead!
