DevNQ Events

Events represent an important and memorable way to bring people together, teach new skills and strenthen our community. At DevNQ and we're committed to hosting only the best of them for North Queensland's developers.

Meetups

DevNQ Meetups are different; we strive to find high quality speakers, panelists and topics that are relevant for software developers, designers, founders and entrepreneurs of all levels. We host monthly meetups where you can learn new things, network with professionals and make valuable industry contacts.

Where possible events are run in-person and livestreamed. Join these events from anywhere, and even catch up on speakers you missed. Check out our Youtube channel for upcoming streams and past recordings.

Every Month
Location
Townsville

Free

Code Club

Code Club provides opportunities for individuals to work on projects, learn from others, and solve programming problems together. Every Tuesday participants get the chance to sharpen their skillsets, and get assistance with hard problems by working on projects in a social setting with other programmers of varying experience and skills. Code club attendees are welcome to bring along their own project, or find a collaborative project they can work on with others.

Student & Industry Meet and Greets

The transition into the workforce can be daunting for young programming professionals. DevNQ hosts Student and Industry Meet and Greets once a year, the goal of which is twofold: introduce aspiring developers into North Queensland's thriving technology ecosystem, and provide new opportunities for networking between current students and prospective employers.

Every July or August
